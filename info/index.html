Bio

Jooho Yoon is currently a software engineer at Vision, Search & Clova, NAVER Corporation. He received B.S. and M.S. in the school of Electrical Engineering , both at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in Feb 2013 and Feb 2015 respectively. During his master's program, he was advised by Prof. Yung Yi. He was a member of Laboratory of LeArning in Networking: Algorithm, Design and Analysis (LANADA). After graduation, he joined the substitutional military service at TmaxSoft in Feb 2015- Jan 2017. He fulfilled his military service at NAVER in Feb 2017- Mar 2018.

Research Interests

His primary research interests emphasize on economic aspect of wireless networks and virtual datacenters since fourth year of KAIST. During his military service, most of his works were on UNIX kernel/system specifically network driver/configuration on FreeBSD. After he joined NAVER, he started to work on computer vision and deploying them into market: topics include image retrieval with hand-crafted/learning-based local descriptors, image quality assessment and image attribute extraction. All of them are released successfully onto the global marketplace. His current research interests include:
